# Launch Strategy

> Continuous launches across product lifecycle for sustained momentum.

## Trigger

`/launch` or "plan a launch" or "launch strategy for"

## Core Philosophy

> "The best companies don't just launch once—they launch again and again."

Success = Phased momentum-building, not single-event announcements.

## ORB Channel Framework

### Owned Channels (Direct access)
- Email lists
- Blog/website
- Podcast
- Community (Discord, Slack)
- Product (in-app)

**Benefits:** No algorithm dependency, compound value over time

### Rented Channels (Algorithm-dependent)
- Social media (Twitter, LinkedIn, TikTok)
- App stores
- YouTube
- Reddit

**Strategy:** Drive traffic toward owned ecosystem

### Borrowed Channels (Third-party audiences)
- Guest content
- Podcast appearances
- Collaborations
- Influencer partnerships
- Speaking engagements

**Strategy:** Proactive outreach with win-win positioning

## Five-Phase Launch

### Phase 1: Internal Launch
- Recruit early testers (team, friends, advisors)
- Gather feedback on core value prop
- Fix critical bugs
- Validate messaging

### Phase 2: Alpha Launch
- External users (small group)
- Landing page with waitlist
- Email capture begins
- Early testimonials

### Phase 3: Beta Launch
- Scaled testing
- Influencer recruitment
- Content creation
- Community building

### Phase 4: Early Access
- Data gathering at scale
- Messaging refinement
- Pricing validation
- Support process testing

### Phase 5: Full Launch
- Self-serve signups
- All channels activated
- Press/media outreach
- Paid acquisition (if ready)

## Launch Checklist

### Pre-Launch (2-4 weeks before)

**Assets:**
- [ ] Landing page live
- [ ] Email capture working
- [ ] Demo video/screenshots
- [ ] Press kit/media assets
- [ ] Social media graphics
- [ ] Launch announcement drafted

**Relationships:**
- [ ] Influencer outreach started
- [ ] Press contacts identified
- [ ] Community partners aligned
- [ ] Beta users ready to amplify

**Technical:**
- [ ] Signup flow tested
- [ ] Onboarding complete
- [ ] Support system ready
- [ ] Analytics tracking live

### Launch Day

**Morning:**
- [ ] Email to waitlist/list
- [ ] Blog post published
- [ ] Social posts scheduled
- [ ] In-app announcement (if applicable)
- [ ] Team aligned on response duties

**All Day:**
- [ ] Monitor all channels
- [ ] Respond to every comment
- [ ] Share user feedback/wins
- [ ] Direct traffic to owned channels
- [ ] Document everything

**Evening:**
- [ ] Thank you posts
- [ ] Day 1 metrics captured
- [ ] Team debrief
- [ ] Tomorrow's plan set

### Post-Launch (1-4 weeks after)

- [ ] Automated onboarding sequences
- [ ] Follow-up emails to signups
- [ ] Comparison pages created
- [ ] Case studies from early users
- [ ] Staggered feature announcements

## Product Hunt Strategy

### Pre-Launch
- Build relationships with hunters/influencers
- Study top launches in your category
- Optimize listing (tagline, images, description)
- Prepare team for all-day engagement
- Schedule for Tuesday-Thursday

### Launch Day
- Post at 12:01 AM PT
- Treat as all-day engagement event
- Respond to EVERY comment
- Share updates throughout day
- Direct traffic to email capture

### Listing Optimization
```
Tagline: [Benefit] for [Audience] (max 60 chars)
First comment: Founder story + why you built it
Images: GIF of product in action, screenshots
```

### Post-Hunt
- Convert visitors to email subscribers
- Follow up with supporters
- Gather feedback from comments
- Maintain momentum with updates

## Channel-Specific Tactics

### Email
```
Subject: [Product] is live 🚀

[Personal opening]

After X months, [Product] is ready.

[What it does - 1 sentence]

[Key benefit 1]
[Key benefit 2]
[Key benefit 3]

[CTA Button]

[Social proof if available]

[Closing]
```

### Twitter/X
```
Thread format:

1/ [Announcement + hook]

We've been working on this for X months.

Today, [Product] is live.

2/ [The problem it solves]

3/ [How it works]

4/ [Key feature 1]

5/ [Key feature 2]

6/ [Social proof/early results]

7/ [CTA + link]

RT the first tweet if this is useful 🙏
```

### LinkedIn
```
[Hook - personal story or bold claim]

[2-3 paragraphs: problem, solution, why now]

[Key results or differentiators as bullets]

[CTA]

[Engagement question]
```

## Momentum Tactics

### Pre-Launch Hype
- Countdown posts
- Behind-the-scenes content
- Early access invites
- Teaser features

### Launch Week
- Daily updates/stories
- User spotlight each day
- Feature deep-dives
- Q&A sessions

### Post-Launch
- Weekly feature releases
- User success stories
- Milestone celebrations
- Roadmap previews

## Case Studies

### Superhuman
- Invite-only access
- Personalized onboarding demos
- Word-of-mouth through exclusivity
- Waitlist of 275,000+

### Notion
- Twitter/Reddit virality
- Template marketplace
- Community-driven content
- Funneled to email signup

### TRMNL
- Free hardware to YouTubers
- Snazzy Labs video: 500K+ views
- Authentic integration, not ad read

## Metrics to Track

| Phase | Key Metrics |
|-------|-------------|
| Pre-launch | Waitlist size, email open rates |
| Launch day | Signups, traffic sources, engagement |
| Week 1 | Activation rate, retention, feedback |
| Month 1 | Conversion, churn, NPS |

## Anti-Patterns

- Single big-bang launch (no phases)
- Launching before product is ready
- Ignoring owned channels for rented
- No follow-up after launch day
- Not responding to feedback
- Launching on Friday/weekend
