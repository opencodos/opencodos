# Social Content

> Build audiences and drive engagement across social platforms.

## Trigger

`/social` or "write a LinkedIn post" or "create a Twitter thread"

## Platform Strategy

| Platform | Best For | Frequency | Format |
|----------|----------|-----------|--------|
| LinkedIn | B2B thought leadership | 3-5x/week | Stories, insights, data |
| Twitter/X | Real-time, community | 3-10x/day | Short takes, threads |
| Instagram | Visual brands | 1-2 feed + 3-10 Stories/day | Reels, carousels |
| TikTok | Viral education | 1-4x/day | Native, unpolished |
| Facebook | Community, local | 1-2x/day | Discussion, groups |

## Content Pillars (3-5 per brand)

| Pillar | % | Example Topics |
|--------|---|----------------|
| Industry insights | 30% | Trends, analysis, predictions |
| Behind-the-scenes | 25% | Process, failures, learnings |
| Educational | 25% | How-tos, frameworks, tips |
| Personal stories | 15% | Journey, values, experiences |
| Promotional | 5% | Product, launches, offers |

## Hook Formulas

### Curiosity
```
"I was wrong about [belief]"
"The real reason [outcome] happens"
"Nobody talks about [hidden truth]"
```

### Story-Based
```
"Last week, [unexpected event] happened"
"3 years ago, I [mistake]"
"A customer just told me [surprising thing]"
```

### Value
```
"How to [outcome] without [pain]"
"The [number] [things] that [outcome]"
"Stop doing [common mistake]"
```

### Contrarian
```
"Everyone says [X]. The truth is [Y]"
"Unpopular opinion: [stance]"
"[Common advice] is wrong. Here's why:"
```

## LinkedIn Story Post Template

```
[HOOK - 1-2 lines, creates curiosity]

[SCENE SETTING - Brief context]

[CHALLENGE - What went wrong or was hard]

[ACTIONS TAKEN - What you did]

[TURNING POINT - The insight or change]

[RESULTS - Specific outcomes]

[LESSON - Takeaway for reader]

[ENGAGEMENT QUESTION]
```

**Example:**
```
Last month I lost a $50k deal.

The prospect loved the product.
The demo went perfectly.
They were ready to sign.

Then I made one mistake:
I rushed the contract.

Instead of addressing their legal team's concerns,
I pushed for a quick close.

They went dark. Deal died.

The lesson?
Speed kills deals.

What's a deal you lost by moving too fast?
```

## Twitter Thread Blueprint

```
1/ [Hook with value promise]

2/ [First point - strongest insight]

3/ [Second point with example]

4/ [Third point with data/proof]

...

N/ [Summary + CTA]

If this was helpful:
• Follow @handle for more
• RT the first tweet
• [Link to resource]
```

## Content Calendar

### Weekly Planning
- Monday: Plan week's content
- Batch create: 2-3 hours
- Schedule using native tools or Buffer/Hootsuite
- Reserve 30 min/day for engagement

### Daily Engagement (30 min)
1. Respond to all comments (10 min)
2. Engage with 5-10 target accounts (10 min)
3. Share insights in relevant conversations (5 min)
4. Send 2-3 relationship-building DMs (5 min)

## Repurposing System

**One pillar piece → Multiple posts:**

```
Blog Post (1500 words)
    ↓
LinkedIn article summary
Twitter thread (key points)
Instagram carousel (5 slides)
TikTok video (1 main point)
5 individual tweets (quotes)
Email newsletter section
```

## Reverse Engineering Framework

1. **Identify** 10-20 high-engagement creators in niche
2. **Collect** 500+ posts for analysis
3. **Analyze** quantitatively (engagement, formats, timing)
4. **Analyze** qualitatively (hooks, emotions, CTAs)
5. **Document** repeatable patterns
6. **Apply** with your voice and specifics

## Voice Principles

- Write conversationally (read aloud test)
- Use specifics over generalities
- Short sentences create rhythm
- One idea per paragraph
- Break grammar rules intentionally
- Authenticity over polish

## Converting Attention to Action

### Soft CTAs
- Newsletter signup
- Free resource download
- Follow for more
- Save this post

### Direct CTAs
- Product link
- Book a call
- Join waitlist
- Buy now

**Ratio:** 80% value, 20% ask

## Analytics Focus

| Metric | Why It Matters |
|--------|----------------|
| Engagement rate | Content resonance |
| Comments | Conversation quality |
| Shares/RTs | Virality potential |
| Saves | Value perception |
| Link clicks | Conversion intent |

**Weekly review:** Top 3 and bottom 3 posts. What patterns emerge?

## Quick Wins

- First line = 80% of success
- Post when your audience is online
- Engage in comments for 30 min after posting
- Use native features (polls, carousels, threads)
- Be consistent > be perfect
