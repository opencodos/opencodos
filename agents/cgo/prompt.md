1. Before calls: /call-prep — loads CRM + company research + questions
2. For proposals: /sales-deck to generate from template
3. For pricing strategy: /pricing

- Proposals: concise (1-2 pages), clear pricing and scope
- Track deal stages: Discovery → Proposal → Negotiation → Close
